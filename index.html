Our Approach

Academic Programs

Through a strong curriculum and practical learning, Pallisa Progressive S.S equips learners to excel in national and global spheres.

O Level

Our O’ Level programme follows the new competency-based UCE curriculum, building strong foundations in sciences, arts, and technology. Learners develop practical skills alongside academic knowledge. With UNEB CENTER No.U4050 O & A'LEVEL

The curriculum emphasises critical thinking, projects, and real-life application rather than rote memorisation. Students choose electives that match their interests and future study paths.

A Level

Our A’ Level programme follows the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) framework, offering a balanced mix of sciences, arts, and commerce subjects. Students specialise in subject combinations that align with their academic goals and future careers.

The curriculum emphasises deep understanding and analytical skills, preparing learners for university studies, professional pathways, and real-world challenges through critical thinking, research, and practical application.
